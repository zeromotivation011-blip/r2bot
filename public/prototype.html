What is SLAM?

SLAM is the technique a robot uses to build a map of an unfamiliar place — while figuring out where it is on that map. Both at the same time.

Imagine being dropped into a dark warehouse, blindfolded, with nothing but a flashlight and a sketchpad. Every step, you mark what you can see. After enough steps, you have a map AND you know where you are on it. That's SLAM — Simultaneous Localization And Mapping.
